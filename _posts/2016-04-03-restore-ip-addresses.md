## 93. 复原IP地址 (Medium)

<p><strong>示例:</strong></p>

<pre><strong>输入:</strong> &quot;25525511135&quot;
<strong>输出:</strong> <code>[&quot;255.255.11.135&quot;, &quot;255.255.111.35&quot;]</code></pre>
