## 590. N叉树的后序遍历 (Easy)

{% raw %}

<p>例如，给定一个&nbsp;<code>3叉树</code>&nbsp;:</p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p><img src="https://assets.leetcode-cn.com/aliyun-lc-upload/uploads/2018/10/12/narytreeexample.png" style="width: 100%; max-width: 300px;"></p>

<p>&nbsp;</p>

<p>返回其后序遍历: <code>[5,6,3,2,4,1]</code>.</p>
